# Design Guidelines

## Engage Identity

- Agility should generally have the strongest immediate-damage Engage actions.
- Strength Engage actions should lean toward forceful setup, durability tradeoffs, or sustained physical advantage.
- Intelligence Engage actions should lean toward spells, control, resource setup, and healing over time.
- Exceptions are welcome when an item has a clear cost or build requirement, but the distinction should remain visible across the reward pool.

## Mordrake Visual Bible

Mordrake is an ambiguously ageless legendary hero corrupted by The Black Crown. He should look powerful and heroic before he looks monstrous: broad armored shoulders, noble face, long white-and-grey hair, an ice-cold stare, and visible strain from a troubled mind. Corruption should show around the eyes, temples, veins, and armor seams.

The Black Crown is dark metal with tall jagged spikes and red engravings. It must read immediately as the crown of a dark lord, partly fused to Mordrake rather than simply worn.

His armor is made from blackened metal and consuming dark magic. It should feel impressive, ancient, and ruinous, with smoky seams, ember-red cracks, and red rune engravings. The silhouette should remain consistent across future images: spiked crown, long pale hair, broad dark armor, ragged cloak or mantle, and a defensive warning posture when he first appears.
